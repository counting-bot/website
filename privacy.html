PRIVACY POLICY

1. Updating Data

The stored data may be updated when using commands, voting on bot listing stites, or by becoming a donator. Updating data will require the action of a user. We may store any sort of Snowflake IDs provided by Discord and user input associated with our bot's features. We will not knowingly store sensitive data, identifying data, unnecessary data, or data which is not used in our bot's features. By interacting with our bot, you authorize us to store said data.

2. Removal of Data

Users may manually request this data to be deleted. Additionally, users who own or maintain said server can delete data using the associated command, or by making a request in the support server. We may ask for verification of ownership of the Server.
